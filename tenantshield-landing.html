FAQ
Common questions

Is this actual legal advice?

No. TenantShield provides legal information and analysis based on New York statutes and case law. We are not a law firm and do not create an attorney-client relationship. For complex cases, we recommend consulting with a tenant rights attorney — and our analysis can help you have a more informed conversation with one.

Is my data safe?

Yes. Your case details are analyzed in real time and never stored on our servers. We do not sell, share, or monetize your data. All data is encrypted in transit using TLS. You can request deletion of any submitted data at any time.

Can I cancel my Pro subscription anytime?

Absolutely. Cancel anytime with one click — no questions asked, no hoops to jump through. Your Pro access continues until the end of your billing period. Plus, you get a full 7-day free trial to try everything risk-free.

What areas of NYC law does TenantShield cover?

TenantShield covers New York Real Property Law (including warranty of habitability and anti-retaliation protections), HSTPA 2019 rent stabilization rules, NYC Administrative Code housing provisions, NYC Housing Court procedures, and security deposit laws. We cover illegal lockouts, rent overcharges, repair issues, harassment, lease reviews, and deposit disputes.

How accurate is the AI analysis?

Our AI agents are trained on current New York tenant protection statutes and reference specific sections of the law. The three-agent debate format catches blind spots that a single analysis would miss. However, every case is unique — use our analysis as a starting point, especially for complex situations.
